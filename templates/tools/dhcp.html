DHCP networks

Reschedule networks between DHCP agents — the companion to the L3 router tool when you're decommissioning a network node. DHCP and L3 scheduling are independent in Neutron, so a full drain means moving both. Networks are removed from the source agent and then added to the target. A brief DHCP outage in the gap is usually harmless (existing leases keep working) but it can pinch PXE-booting hosts.
